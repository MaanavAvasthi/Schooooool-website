Campus Life

School Church

Was established in 1978

The church is a place of worship for christians. It is considered as a place of god. It is a holy building for christian religious activity. People gather at church and pray to god. They light candles in front of Jesus. There are two main missions of every church One mission is to develop Christ- like attributes in mens lives. Second mission is to transform society to a better peaceful place to live. There is a saint in every church called “Father”. Every year on the eve of Christmas, the church is decorated with flowers, bells etc.





School Library
